1. Introduction

Welcome to Sure ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

We collect your device's location data solely to:

  • Calculate accurate prayer times for your location
  • Determine Qibla direction
  • Show prayer times for your selected city

Important: This data is processed locally on your device and is NOT transmitted to our servers.

2.2 Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our app, including:

  • App features used

This data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify you personally.

2.3 Subscription Information

If you subscribe to our premium features, Apple handles all payment processing. We only receive confirmation of your subscription status, not your payment details.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

4. Data Storage

These services have their own privacy policies that govern their use of your information.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disable location services at any time through your device settings
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • Manage notification preferences within the app
  • Request information about your data by contacting us

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages and contains religious content appropriate for family use.

8. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Since we don't store personal data on our servers, the risk of data breaches is minimized.
