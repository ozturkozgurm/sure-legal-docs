❤️ Our Mission

Sure is designed to help Muslims stay connected with their faith through accurate prayer times, easy Quran access, and meaningful reminders.

We believe technology should serve spiritual growth and bring peace to daily life. Our goal is to create a beautiful, reliable, and respectful app that supports your daily Islamic practices.

⭐ Features

  • 🕌 Accurate prayer times for your location
  • 📖 Complete Quran with 6,236 verses and translations
  • 🧭 Qibla compass for prayer direction
  • 🔔 Customizable prayer notifications
  • 🌙 Inspirational Friday messages
  • ❤️ Save and like your favorite verses
